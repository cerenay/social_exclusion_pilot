Starting this second stage, the 10 points you earned from working on the task in the previous stage and the 10 points three other participants earned each are pooled together. During this stage you and the other three participants will determine how this total of 40 earned points are going to be distributed amongst the four of you. Moreover each participant will be assigned a player number and an identifying color group which is either Green group or Purple group.

{% if player.colour == "Green" %} You have been assigned to the colour: Green. In this stage, you are matched with 3 other players, 2 of them are in the other Purple group and 1 is in the same gorup as you, Green group. {% endif %} {% if player.colour == "Purple" %} You have been assigned to the colour: Purple. In this stage, you are matched with 3 other players, 2 of them are in the other Green group and 1 is in the same group as you, Purple Group. {% endif %} You are now going to make some decisions for 20 rounds with the same 3 players. The stage will consist of 20 periods where in each period (except the last) one player will make a decision.

In each round, one player is assigned the role of “Beneficiary”. The player who is assigned to be a Beneficiary of a round will:

  • Receive 2 out of 40 points, which will be added to their individual bonus point total.
  • Select who of the 3 other players gets to have the role of “Beneficiary” in the next round.
  • For the first round it will be randomly determined who becomes the Beneficiary, and the Beneficiary in the last round (round 20) will not get to choose any next Beneficiary.
