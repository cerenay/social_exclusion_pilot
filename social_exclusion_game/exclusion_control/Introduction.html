Starting this second stage, the 10 points you earned from working on the task in the previous stage and the 10 points three other players earned each are pooled together. During this stage the computer will determine how this total of 40 earned points are going to be distributed amongst the four of you. Moreover each participant will be assigned a player number from 1 to 4.

You are now going to be in Stage 2 for 20 rounds with the same 3 players. The stage will consist of 20 periods where in each period one player is randomly selected as the "Beneficiary" by the computer. In each stage, computer will reveal the random choice when you click the "Next" button.

In each round, one player is assigned the role of “Beneficiary”. The player who is assigned to be a Beneficiary of a round will:

  • Receive 2 out of 40 points, which will be added to their individual bonus point total.
