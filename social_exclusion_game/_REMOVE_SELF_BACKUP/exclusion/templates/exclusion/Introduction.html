In this stage, you are matched with 3 other players, 2 of them are in the other Green group and 1 is in the same group as you, Purple Group. {% endif %} You are now going to make some decisions for 20 rounds with the same 3 players.

Your earnings from Stage 1 have been added to the earnings produced by the other 3 players to make the combined earnings. The combined earnings are 40 points. You are going to interact with the other 3 players for 20 rounds and this interaction will determine how the combined earnings from the previously completed task will be distributed to all 4 players.

In each round, one player of the team is assigned the role of “Beneficiary”. The player who is assigned to be a Beneficiary of a round will:

  • Receive 2 out of 40 points, which will be added to their individual bonus point total.
  • Select who of the 3 other players gets to have the role of “Beneficiary” in the next round.
  • For the first round it will be randomly determined who becomes the Beneficiary, and the Beneficiary in the last round (round 20) will not get to choose any next Beneficiary.
